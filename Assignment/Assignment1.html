Grandma's Chocolate Cake

A Delicious homemade dessert!

This Chocolate cake has been in our family for generations.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C
  2. Mix all dry ingredients

Cooking Info:

Prep Time Cook Time Servings Difficulty
20 min 30 min 8 Easy

Chef's Tips:

Tip 1: Use high-quality cocoa powder.

Tip 2: Let the cake cool before frosting.
